Eco Zoom Festival 2025

Promoting climate awareness and sustainable practices through education and entertainment.

About the Festival

The Eco Zoom Festival is a global initiative focused on addressing climate change through outreach, education, and entertainment. Under the theme "Climate Change: A Global Responsibility," this festival aims to raise awareness about the impact of climate change and inspire collective action to create a sustainable future.

Taking place on World Green Day, the festival will be hosted in multiple locations including the USA, Canada, Europe, Asia, the UK, and the Gulf. It will feature interactive workshops, educational sessions, and vibrant cultural performances to engage communities and promote sustainable living practices.

Our Goals

  • Raise global awareness about the impact of climate change.
  • Promote sustainable practices and green technologies.
  • Foster community involvement in climate action initiatives.

Festival Activities

  • Educational workshops on climate change and sustainability.
  • Panel discussions with environmental experts and activists.
  • Cultural performances celebrating nature and the environment.
  • Interactive exhibits showcasing green technologies and solutions.

How You Can Participate

Join the Eco Zoom Festival in raising awareness and promoting sustainable practices. Your support and involvement are key to addressing climate change.
