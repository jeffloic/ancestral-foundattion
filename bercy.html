Bercy International Fashion Show

Uniting Africa and France through a fusion of traditional and contemporary fashion.

About the Cause

The beauty of broken cultures is beyond the display of various cultural elements but rather unifying the diaspora of multicultures to share and learn from one another is the ultimate. Nothing brings people together than what they all share in common and culture is universal which makes it a powerful tool to preach the message of universal peace. Each country has a unique cultural heritage cherish and uphold by the people, therefore all Afro-Diasporans including all modern and traditional institutions are our key partners to make this festival a success. The Broken Cultures Festival will seek a cultural representation from all African consulates to display, share and educate us on their cultural heritages centered on peace and development. Traditional and cultural costumes will be display by all representatives from various groups and also the consulates inviting some of their musical and cultural acts to share their crafts with the world. The festival will rotate in various countries across the world but annually in the United States or Ghana partnering with all the foreign consulates to established same in their countries to promote development and peaceful coexistence among all Afro-Diasporans especially Ghana and the United States. Establishing a mutual cultural and business interrelation among Afro-Diaspora and Africa to promote socioeconomic independent and development in support of black empowerment and excellence.
