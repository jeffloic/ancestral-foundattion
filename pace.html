PACE Avenue Artisan Center

Empowering youth with technical and artisanal skills to reduce migration and foster economic growth.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Providing technological, technical, and artisanal skills to youth in urban and rural communities, equipping them with global job market ethics, and inviting resource mentors to guide their training. The initiative incorporates workshops across diverse fields to integrate technology into the curriculum and foster technological advancement.

Vision: Establishing a modern, practical-oriented institution with accommodation facilities, training apprentices in technical and artisanal fields, and securing international contracts for final-year trainees to support their growth and self-sufficiency.

Objectives

  • Reduce rural-urban migration among youth in rural communities.
  • Build a skilled youth population to drive national industrialization.
  • Provide benchmark job market skills by training a highly skilled workforce.
  • Minimize dependency on government jobs and reduce graduate unemployment.

Key Features

  • After-school technical and artisanal training for secondary and university students.
  • Workshops focusing on managerial skills and technological integration.
  • Regional pilot programs to evaluate scalability across other regions.
  • Partnerships with international organizations for apprenticeships and contracts.

Impact

By training 1,000 to 5,000 youths in its first year, the PACE Avenue Artisan Center will create a skilled workforce equipped with both technical and managerial skills. This initiative will foster economic growth, reduce migration to urban areas, and address youth unemployment challenges.

How You Can Help

Join us in empowering juveniles and promoting a crime-free future. Your contributions and participation will make a lasting impact on their lives and our communities.
