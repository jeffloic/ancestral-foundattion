Carnival of Folk Cultures

Preserving and celebrating global folk cultures to foster unity and cultural diplomacy.

About the Carnival

The global cultural space is fascinating with beautiful heritages kept for years and a land of multicultural people tolerant with diversity, inclusiveness, vibrant and colorful atmosphere which promotes global cultural diplomacy to foster peaceful coexistence and social cohesion. Culture is a universal tool for global unification, economic growth, social coherence, and psychological enlightenment to form global cultural conglomerates in shaping up the cultural evolution of strong demand for cultural civilization which facilitates peaceful coexistence in a multicultural environment.

The Sustainable Development Goals of UNESCO on cultural heritage is to preserve both tangible and intangible cultural heritage of the indigenous people which is evidently adrift in our modern society, making some folk cultures endangered legacies to be erased. As the global cultural education percentage ratio keeps declining each year, it is important for global efforts by cultural communities, organizations, civil societies, institutions, and government bodies to create collaborative long-term avenues, programs, projects, and policies that propel cultural education.

In our quest to unify the world with cultural diplomacy, the Carnival of Folk Cultures aims to integrate all cultures under one platform to share, witness, and exchange premium cultural experiences. This event celebrates global broken cultures scattered across the world, uniting them as one big culture each year. The goal is to eliminate cultural and political barriers that restrict the impact of cultural diplomacy in promoting peaceful coexistence and social cohesion, which can be channeled to resolve hostile situations where political solutions are erosive.

Our Goals

  • Preserve endangered folk cultures and promote cultural education.
  • Foster peaceful coexistence and social cohesion through cultural diplomacy.
  • Provide economic opportunities for communities through cultural trade and tourism.

Carnival Highlights

  • Global folk performances showcasing diverse cultural traditions.
  • Workshops and discussions on cultural heritage preservation.
  • Exhibitions of traditional crafts, music, and cuisine from participating countries.
  • Opportunities for cultural products and services to address various needs.

How You Can Participate

Join us in celebrating and preserving the beauty of folk cultures. Your support and participation can make this carnival a global success.
