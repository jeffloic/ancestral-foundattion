The One Dollar Project

Empowering young minds through reading, problem-solving, and innovative thinking to build a better future.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To engage and empower the younger generation to "Read Beyond Culture," fostering an environment where children develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovative abilities. This initiative seeks grants and support to implement programs that encourage children to research, identify, and propose solutions to community challenges.

Vision: To cultivate a generation that is oriented toward problem-solving and innovation. By engaging young minds early, the project aims to build a resourceful society capable of tackling its challenges and improving lives through innovative solutions.

Objectives

  • Encourage children to take interest in researching beyond their immediate interests.
  • Establish "Read Beyond Culture Research Clubs" in schools to foster teamwork in solving community problems.
  • Build confidence in young people to tackle issues at the community level through critical thinking and innovation.
  • Promote education that is practical, relevant, and solution-oriented for a better future.

Project Activities

  • Organize tours to schools nationwide to establish "Read Beyond Culture Research Clubs."
  • Guide students in selecting community issues to research and propose solutions.
  • Provide mentorship and resources to facilitate research and innovative thinking.
  • Host workshops and presentations to showcase student findings and solutions.

Impact

The One Dollar Project empowers children to think critically and solve real-world problems, fostering a resourceful and innovative society. By investing in young minds, this initiative builds the foundation for a brighter, self-reliant future.

How You Can Participate
